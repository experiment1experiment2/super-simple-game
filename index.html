Unknown Figures

Welcome to Unknown Figures! The game where you have to guess the number that the bots will pick. Here are the rules:

  • You have 60 seconds to make your selection.
  • The bots will each pick a number between 0 and 100.
  • Your number will be added to the average of the bot numbers.
  • The player whose number is closest to 80% of the average of all numbers wins.
